Hello! My name is Calvin Cheah and this year will mark my second year in college. My expected graduation date is 2025 and I am a Computer Science major from San Diego, California at Georgia Tech!

I'm passionate about understanding and supporting individuals with autism. Before I attended Georgia Tech I regularly volunteered for Aceing Autism at Barnes Tennis Center in San Diego. Aceing Autism hosts tennis clinics specifically designed to be more accesable for those on the autism spectrum. I personally enjoy taking the time to understand my students so that we both can work together to improve their skills.
